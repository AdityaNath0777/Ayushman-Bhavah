About Us

Our Unified Healthcare Platform provides up-to-date information on available beds. Users can locate nearby facilities, view their capacities, and book slots . Video consultancy allows remote consultations with doctors.

Our solution reduces unnecessary travel, benefiting patients, healthcare providers, and the environment. The uniqueness lies in its holistic approach, integrating real-time data, appointment booking, and video consultancy. Future scope includes expanding to more regions, electronic health record integration, and Al-driven personalized recommendations.
